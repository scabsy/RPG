when creating a map you will use single characters for each square of the grid
A - starting player point. the most right is the only one that will be used. un-used A's will also prevent the NPC's and the player from moving

a - water
b - grass
c - rock
d - tree
e - bridge

buildings
f - left side window
g - right side window
h - left side door
i - right side door
j - centre door
k - centre door
l - single building
inside buildings
m - floor
n - counter
o - counter end l
p - counter end r
q - counter ud l
r - counter ud r
